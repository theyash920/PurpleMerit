COURSE: MATH51
TITLE: Linear Algebra, Multivariable Calculus, and Modern Applications

LEVEL: undergraduate

DESCRIPTION:
Unified coverage of linear algebra and multivariable differential calculus, emphasizing connections between them and applications to engineering and computer science. Topics include: vectors, matrices, systems of linear equations, eigenvalues and eigenvectors, partial derivatives, gradients, Jacobians, optimization, and linear regression. This course satisfies the mathematics requirement for the BS in Computer Science degree.
