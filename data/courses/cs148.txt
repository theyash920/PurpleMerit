TYPE: course

COURSE: CS148
TITLE: Introduction to Computer Graphics and Imaging

LEVEL: undergraduate

DESCRIPTION:
Fundamental concepts in computer graphics: rendering pipelines, modeling, textures, shading, ray tracing, rasterization, clipping, transformations, curves and surfaces, and animation. Students build a ray tracer and implement shader programs. Introduction to GPU programming and real-time rendering techniques.

PREREQUISITES:
CS107

PREREQ_LOGIC:
* CS107 must be completed. MATH51 is recommended for geometric foundations.
