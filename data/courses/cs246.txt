COURSE: CS246
TITLE: Mining Massive Data Sets

LEVEL: undergraduate

DESCRIPTION:
Design of efficient algorithms for processing massive datasets that do not fit in main memory. Topics include MapReduce/Spark, locality-sensitive hashing, dimensionality reduction, recommendation systems, mining social-network graphs, community detection, web search/PageRank, frequent itemsets, and data stream algorithms.

PREREQUISITES:
CS107 AND CS161
