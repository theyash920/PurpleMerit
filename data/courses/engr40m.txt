TYPE: course

COURSE: ENGR40M
TITLE: An Intro to Making: What is EE

LEVEL: undergraduate

DESCRIPTION:
Introduction to electrical engineering through hands-on making projects. Topics include circuits, microcontrollers (Arduino), sensors, actuators, LEDs, and basic signal processing. Students build several projects including a heart-rate monitor and a light display. No prior electrical engineering experience required. This course satisfies the engineering fundamentals requirement.
