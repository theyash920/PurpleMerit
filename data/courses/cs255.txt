TYPE: course

COURSE: CS255
TITLE: Introduction to Cryptography

LEVEL: undergraduate

DESCRIPTION:
Theory and practice of cryptographic techniques used in computer security. Topics include symmetric encryption (block ciphers, stream ciphers, AES), public-key cryptography (RSA, Diffie-Hellman, elliptic curves), digital signatures, authenticated encryption, hash functions, key management, and protocols for secure computation and zero-knowledge proofs.
