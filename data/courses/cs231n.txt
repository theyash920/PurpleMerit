TITLE: Deep Learning for Computer Vision

LEVEL: graduate

DESCRIPTION:
Computer vision has become ubiquitous in our society, with applications in search, image understanding, apps, mapping, medicine, drones, and self-driving cars. Core topics include image classification, object detection, segmentation, generative models (GANs, VAEs), visual question answering, and video understanding. Architectures covered include CNNs, ResNets, Vision Transformers, and modern foundation models.
