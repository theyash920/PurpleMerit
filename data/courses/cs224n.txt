COURSE: CS224N
TITLE: Natural Language Processing with Deep Learning

LEVEL: graduate

DESCRIPTION:
Methods for processing human language information and the fundamentals of modern NLP using deep learning. Topics include word vectors, recurrent neural networks, attention mechanisms, transformer architectures, pre-training and fine-tuning (BERT, GPT), machine translation, question answering, text generation, and large language models. Students complete a significant final project.
