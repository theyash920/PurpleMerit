TYPE: course

COURSE: CS229
TITLE: Machine Learning

LEVEL: undergraduate

DESCRIPTION:
Topics include supervised learning (generative/discriminative learning, parametric/non-parametric learning, neural networks, support vector machines), unsupervised learning (clustering, dimensionality reduction, kernel methods), learning theory (bias/variance tradeoffs, practical optimization), reinforcement learning, and control. Students implement learning algorithms and apply them to real-world datasets.

PREREQUISITES:
CS109 AND MATH51

PREREQ_LOGIC:
* Both CS109 and MATH51 must be completed. CS106B is strongly recommended.
