TYPE: course

COURSE: CS144
TITLE: Introduction to Computer Networking

LEVEL: undergraduate

DESCRIPTION:
Principles and practice of computer networking, with emphasis on the Internet. Topics include layered architectures, TCP/IP protocol suite, HTTP, DNS, routing algorithms, congestion control, reliable transport, network security, and wireless networking. Students build networking applications and implement protocol components from scratch.
